About KOH

About
KOH Muay Thai

KOH Muay Thai Instructors love, respect, and believe in their Nak Muays.

KOH Muay Thai Lutz strives to continue the traditional Thai methods in each class, paying special attention to emphasize devotion and respect to the participant and the sport as well. Students, also known as Nak Muays, are an integral part of the KOH Muay Thai community and family when they become a member. In turn, the students give each trainer full respect, commitment, and devotion — and it is respected in return.

Muay Thai is an ancient martial art originating from Thailand, dating back to the 16th century. Also known as "The Art of Eight Limbs," Muay Thai combines the use of fists, feet, knees, and elbows to create a powerful and efficient fighting system. Training in Muay Thai helps develop important life skills like perseverance, focus, and confidence.

  • Stances and footwork
  • Punching and kicking techniques
  • Defense and counterattack strategies
  • Conditioning drills and bag work
  • Clinching and takedown techniques
  • Traditional Muay Thai forms and rituals
